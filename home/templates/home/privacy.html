Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to Project 4 (P4) (“we”, “our”, “us”).

We are committed to protecting and respecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal data when you interact with us — whether in-store, online, via our website, mobile services, or customer support.

We process your personal data in accordance with the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 2018.

2. Who We Are

Project 4 (P4) is a supermarket retailer providing groceries, household goods, and related services both in-store and online.

Data Controller:
Project 4 (P4)
123 Supermarket Lane, UK
Email: info@projectfour.com

3. The Data We Collect

We may collect and process the following types of personal data:

a) Information You Provide to Us

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Billing and delivery address
  • Account login details
  • Payment information (processed securely via third-party providers)
  • Customer service enquiries and communications

b) Information We Collect Automatically

  • IP address
  • Device type and browser information
  • Website usage data (pages visited, time spent, clicks)
  • Cookies and tracking data

c) Transaction Data

  • Purchase history
  • Order details
  • Delivery preferences

d) In-Store Data

  • CCTV footage for security and loss prevention
  • Transaction data linked to loyalty or payment methods

4. How We Use Your Data

We use your personal data for the following purposes:

  • To process and deliver your orders
  • To manage your account
  • To provide customer support
  • To improve our products, services, and user experience
  • To personalise your shopping experience
  • To prevent fraud and ensure security
  • To comply with legal and regulatory obligations

Where required, we rely on lawful bases such as:

  • Contractual necessity
  • Legitimate interests
  • Legal obligations
  • Consent (for marketing communications)

5. Marketing Communications

We may send you marketing communications about products, offers, and updates where you have opted in.

You can unsubscribe at any time by:

  • Clicking the “unsubscribe” link in emails
  • Contacting us directly

We will never sell your personal data to third parties for marketing purposes.

6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to improve website functionality, analyse performance and usage, and personalise content and advertising. You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings. For more information, please see our Cookie Policy.

7. Sharing Your Data

We may share your data with trusted third parties, including:

  • Payment processors
  • Delivery and logistics partners
  • IT and system providers
  • Marketing and analytics platforms

All third parties are required to respect the security of your personal data and process it in accordance with the law. We may also disclose your data if required to do so by law or to protect our legal rights.

8. Data Retention

We only retain your personal data for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes we collected it for and to satisfy legal, accounting, or reporting requirements. When your data is no longer required, it will be securely deleted or anonymised.

9. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your data, including secure servers and encryption, restricted access controls, and regular security assessments. While we take all reasonable steps to protect your data, no system is completely secure.

10. Your Rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to or restrict processing
  • Request data portability
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise your rights, please contact us at: info@projectfour.com

11. International Transfers

If we transfer your data outside the UK, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, such as UK-approved standard contractual clauses and adequacy decisions.

12. Third-Party Links

Our website or services may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices, and we encourage you to read their policies.

13. Children’s Privacy

Our services are not intended for individuals under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

14. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

15. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please contact us:
