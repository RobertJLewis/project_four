About Us

Welcome to Project 4 (P4) — more than just a supermarket.

At P4, we’re on a mission to redefine the way people shop for everyday essentials. We believe supermarkets shouldn’t just be places you have to go — they should be places you want to go. That’s why we’ve built a shopping experience that’s faster, smarter, and designed entirely around you.

Our Vision

Project 4 was created with a simple idea: make high-quality food and everyday products more accessible, efficient, and enjoyable.

We’re combining modern technology with carefully curated products to deliver a supermarket experience that fits seamlessly into your lifestyle — whether you’re shopping in-store or online.

What Makes Us Different

  1. Smarter Shopping — we use innovative systems and streamlined layouts to help you find what you need quickly, with no wasted time.
  2. Curated Quality — every product is selected with purpose, focusing on quality, value, and reliability.
  3. Customer First — your convenience and experience drive every decision we make.
  4. Future-Focused — we’re building the supermarket of tomorrow with continuous innovation.

Why “Project 4”?

“Project 4” represents our commitment to building something better — a fourth evolution of retail. A space where technology, quality, convenience, and experience come together as one.

Our Promise

  • Deliver quality you can trust
  • Keep prices fair and transparent
  • Make your shopping experience seamless
  • Continuously innovate and improve
